Join the Trans Victorian Forum

We are a community of transgender folks in and around southern Vancouver Island. Our goal is to provide a welcoming space for socializing and planning meetups in real life. For now Discord is our main platform, but any updates on how to connect with us will always be found here.

We are open to anyone 18 or older who identifies as transgender and lives in or regularly visits the greater Victoria area.

If you are interested, please join our lobby server. We'll ask you some questions and check the vibe, and hopefully invite you in and get to know you.

You can also follow us on Instagram @transvictorianforum to stay up to date on important announcements.
